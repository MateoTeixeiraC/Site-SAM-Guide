Interactive Game

Here is a running track. You control the character on it using the arrow keys. The goal of the game is to complete a full lap of the track; you will receive audio signals when you approach an edge and need to turn, a high-pitched sound for the left edge and a low-pitched sound for the right edge. If you do not have a visual impairment, feel free to close your eyes for more immersion.

This mini-game is inspired by the experiments conducted at the Sotteville stadium by the LITIS team. It does not faithfully reproduce the final system of the project but offers a simplified and interactive version to better grasp the main principles: moving, orienting oneself, staying on a defined track using sensory signals. A playful way to approach the concrete challenges of the SAM-Guide project.
